Items We Cannot Accept

For safety and legal reasons, we cannot remove: Hazardous materials, chemicals, paint, asbestos, medical waste, explosives, ammunition, or radioactive materials. Contact us if you have questions about specific items.

How Our Service Works

1. Contact Us

Call, email, or fill out our online form. Tell us what you need removed and when you need it done. We'll provide a free estimate based on volume and type of items.

2. Schedule Pickup

We'll schedule a convenient time for pickup. Same-day or next-day service is often available. We'll confirm your appointment and arrival window.

3. We Do the Heavy Lifting

Our team arrives on time, loads everything into our truck, and cleans up the area. You just point to what needs to go - we handle all the heavy lifting and loading.

4. Responsible Disposal

We dispose of items responsibly, recycling and donating when possible. We take pride in keeping usable items out of landfills and protecting the environment.
