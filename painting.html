Our Painting Process

1. Consultation & Quote

We start with a thorough assessment of your project. We'll discuss your vision, inspect surfaces, take measurements, and provide a detailed quote with no hidden fees.

2. Preparation

Proper prep is crucial for lasting results. We protect your furniture and floors, repair any damage, clean all surfaces, and apply primer where needed.

3. Professional Application

Our skilled painters apply high-quality paints using professional techniques. We ensure even coverage, clean lines, and smooth finishes throughout your space.

4. Final Inspection & Cleanup

We conduct a thorough walkthrough with you, touch up any areas if needed, and leave your space spotless. Your satisfaction is our guarantee.
