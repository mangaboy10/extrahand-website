Common Drywall Problems We Fix

Water Damage

Water stains, bubbling, or crumbling drywall from leaks or moisture. We assess the damage, ensure proper drying, replace affected sections, and finish to match existing walls.

Impact Holes

Holes from doorknobs, furniture, accidents, or renovations. Whether it's a small punch or a large opening, we patch and repair to restore your wall's integrity.

Cracks & Settlement

Cracks appearing at corners, seams, or along walls due to settling or structural movement. We repair and reinforce to prevent recurring issues.

Poor Previous Work

Visible seams, uneven surfaces, or poorly finished drywall from previous repairs. We correct bad workmanship and restore walls to professional standards.

Our Drywall Repair Process

1. Assessment & Quote

We inspect the damage, identify underlying issues, and provide a detailed quote. We'll explain the repair process and timeline clearly.

2. Preparation & Protection

We protect your floors and furniture, set up proper ventilation, and prepare the work area. Clean cuts and careful removal of damaged drywall.

3. Repair & Installation

Professional patching, taping, and mudding with multiple coats as needed. We ensure proper adhesion and smooth transitions between old and new material.

4. Finishing & Cleanup

Careful sanding, texture matching, and final smoothing. We clean up all dust and debris, leaving your space ready for painting or immediate use.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does drywall repair take?

Small repairs can be completed in a few hours, but require 24-48 hours for the compound to fully dry before painting. Larger repairs may take 2-3 days with drying time between coats.

Can you match my wall texture?

Yes! We're experienced in matching various textures including knockdown, orange peel, and skip trowel. We'll test the texture on a sample area before applying to ensure a perfect match.

Do I need to repaint after repair?

For most repairs, yes. The repaired area will need priming and painting to match your wall color. We can prime the area for you, or provide full painting services if needed.

What about mold or water damage?

We assess for mold and moisture issues before repairing. If mold is present or the source of water damage isn't fixed, we'll recommend addressing those issues first to prevent future problems.

Can you repair ceiling drywall?

Absolutely. We handle ceiling repairs including sagging, cracks, water stains, and texture matching. Ceiling work may require additional time due to access and drying requirements.

Will the repair be noticeable?

Our goal is invisible repairs. With proper technique, texture matching, and finishing, the repair should blend seamlessly. After painting, it should be virtually undetectable.
